Prayer Requests

The St. Bernadette Parish family believes deeply in the power of communal prayer. When we join our voices together in prayer, we create a powerful chorus of faith that reaches the heart of God. We invite you to share your prayer intentions with our parish community.

"Therefore I tell you, whatever you ask for in prayer, believe that you have received it, and it will be yours."

Mark 11:24

Submit Your Prayer Request

Thank you! Your prayer request has been received.

Our Prayer Request Guidelines

  • Your personal information will remain confidential unless you specify otherwise.
  • Prayer requests are reviewed by our pastoral staff before being shared with prayer ministry teams.
  • Mass intentions require a customary offering. Our office will contact you with details.
  • We typically keep intentions in our parish prayers for one month unless otherwise requested.
  • For immediate pastoral care needs, please call the parish office directly.
