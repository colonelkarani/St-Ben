Serve Our Parish & Community

At St. Bernadette Catholic Church, we believe that each person is blessed with unique gifts and talents that can be used to build up the Body of Christ and serve those in need. We invite you to prayerfully consider how God might be calling you to serve in our parish community.

"Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others, as faithful stewards of God's grace in its various forms."

1 Peter 4:10

Liturgical Ministries

Our liturgical ministries enhance our worship experience and help us celebrate the Eucharist with reverence and joy. From proclaiming the Word of God to assisting at the altar, there are many ways to serve in our liturgies.

Extraordinary Ministers of Holy Communion

Assist in the distribution of the Eucharist during Mass and to the homebound. Training provided quarterly.

Contact: Deacon Robert Johnson

Training: First Saturday of each quarter, 9:00 AM

Volunteer

Altar Servers

Assist the priest during Mass. Open to children (4th grade and up), teens, and adults.

Contact: Deacon William Thompson

Faith Formation Ministries

Our faith formation programs help parishioners of all ages grow in their relationship with Christ and understanding of our Catholic faith. These ministries offer opportunities to both learn and teach.

Religious Education Catechists

Share your faith with children in grades K-8. Curriculum and training provided. Commitment is weekly during the school year.

Contact: Maria Gonzalez, DRE

Classes: Sundays, 9:30-10:45 AM or Wednesdays, 6:00-7:15 PM
