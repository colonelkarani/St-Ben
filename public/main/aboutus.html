About Our Parish

Welcome to St. Bernadette Catholic Church, a vibrant Catholic community rooted in faith, compassion, and service. Named after St. Bernadette Soubirous, to whom the Blessed Virgin Mary appeared in Lourdes, France, our parish strives to embody the humility, devotion, and spiritual strength exemplified by our patroness.

Founded in 1958, St. Bernadette has grown from a small mission church to a thriving parish serving hundreds of families in our community. Our beautiful sanctuary, with its stunning stained glass windows depicting the life of St. Bernadette and the apparitions at Lourdes, provides a sacred space for worship, reflection, and communion with God.

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ and to provide a welcoming community where all can encounter Christ's transforming love through the sacraments, prayer, education, and service to others.

St. Bernadette Parish is deeply committed to the Catholic traditions while embracing the calls for renewal articulated by the Second Vatican Council. We seek to be a beacon of hope, healing, and hospitality in our community, where all are welcome to experience the richness of our Catholic faith.

Our parish offers numerous opportunities for spiritual growth, community engagement, and apostolic service. From our excellent religious education programs to our outreach ministries serving the poor and marginalized, we strive to live out our baptismal call to be disciples of Christ in today's world.

"Just as St. Bernadette humbly carried the message of Our Lady to the world, we too are called to be witnesses of Christ's love and mercy in our daily lives, embracing with joy our vocation to holiness and service."

Our Clergy

St. Bernadette Parish is blessed with dedicated clergy who provide spiritual leadership, pastoral care, and sacramental ministry to our community. Get to know the priests and deacons who serve our parish family.

Pastor

Father Thomas has served as our pastor since 2018. With over 25 years of priestly ministry, he brings wisdom, compassion, and a deep love for liturgy to our parish community.

Associate Priests

Rev. James Rodriguez

Associate Pastor

Deacons
