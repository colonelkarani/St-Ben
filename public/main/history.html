Our Humble Beginnings

St Bernadette Catholic Church was founded in 1998 by a small group of devoted families who shared a vision of creating a welcoming spiritual home for their community. In those early days, our congregation of just 23 members gathered in the living room of founding members John and Mary Collins.

With unwavering faith and determination, these founding families pooled their resources to purchase the land where our church still stands today. The first church building was a modest wooden structure, built largely by the hands of our founding members themselves.

Despite its humble size, the church quickly became known for its warm atmosphere and commitment to serving the community. This foundation of faith, service, and fellowship continues to guide our church family to this day.

Our Journey Through Time

1990

The Founding

St Bernadette Catholic Church was founded with 23 members meeting in a living room. The group purchased land and built our first small chapel.

1992

Growing Community

As our congregation grew to over 200 members, we expanded the original building to include additional worship space and classrooms for Sunday School.

1995

New Sanctuary

With continued growth, we broke ground on a new larger sanctuary designed to accommodate our expanding church family and ministry needs.

1998

Community Outreach

Established our Community Care Center to provide food, clothing, and support services to those in need throughout Blessed Hills.

2006

Youth Ministry

Launched our dedicated youth ministry program with the construction of the Youth Activity Center, providing a space for teens to grow in faith.

2005

Modern Expansion

Completed a major renovation and expansion project that gave us our current sanctuary, fellowship hall, and educational facilities.

2018

Digital Ministry

Embraced digital ministry with live-streamed services, an active online community, and digital resources for spiritual growth.

Today

Continuing Our Mission

Today, we continue to grow and serve with over 1,200 members, multiple worship services, and a wide range of ministries that reach throughout our community and beyond.

Our Founding Leaders

Father Paul Mbugua

First Father (1992-2004)

Led our church through its formative years with wisdom and compassion, establishing many traditions we still follow today.

Isaac Kimeu

Founding Members

Opened their home for early worship gatherings and donated the land where our church stands today.

Dorothy Muthoni

Music Director (1952-1975)

Established our church's rich musical tradition and directed our first choir with passion and devotion.

Key Milestones

Building & Growth

From our first wooden chapel to our current sanctuary that seats 800, our physical space has grown alongside our congregation.

Community Impact

Our outreach programs have served over 50,000 individuals in need through food banks, shelter assistance, and counseling services.

Global Missions

We've sponsored mission trips to 17 countries, built 23 wells in Africa, and supported countless families through global partnerships.

Our Continuing Journey

For over 70 years, St Bernadette Catholic Church has been a beacon of hope, faith, and love in Blessed Hills. While we honor our rich history and the foundation laid by those who came before us, we remain focused on the future.

As we look ahead, we're committed to continuing our legacy of meaningful worship, compassionate outreach, and spiritual growth while adapting to meet the needs of our changing world.

We invite you to be part of our ongoing story as we write the next chapter of St Bernadette Catholic Church together.
